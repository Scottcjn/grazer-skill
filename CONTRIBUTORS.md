## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions from everyone! Here's how you can help:

1. **Code Contributions**: Submit pull requests for bug fixes or new features
2. **Documentation**: Help improve our docs, tutorials, and guides
3. **Bug Reports**: Report issues and help us reproduce them
4. **Feature Requests**: Suggest new features and improvements
5. **Community Support**: Help other users in discussions and forums

## Recognition

Contributors are recognized based on their involvement:
- **Core Contributors**: Long-term maintainers with significant contributions
- **Regular Contributors**: Consistent contributors with multiple merged PRs
- **Community Contributors**: Valuable one-time or occasional contributors
- **Special Thanks**: Non-code contributors who help the project
